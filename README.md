# AI Invoice Finance Ops
### n8n + Groq (Free AI) | Automates invoice extraction, approval routing, and finance operations workflows

![Workflow](screenshots/workflow.png)

## Project Summary
AI Invoice Finance Ops is a finance operations automation workflow built with n8n and Groq AI to reduce manual invoice handling, speed up approvals, and improve accuracy in finance processes. The workflow is designed to extract invoice data, structure it, route it through approval steps, notify the right stakeholders, and keep records updated automatically.

This project demonstrates practical finance automation, data extraction workflows, approval routing logic, AI-assisted operations support, and multi-step business process orchestration using low-cost tools.

## The Problem
Finance teams often spend too much time manually reviewing invoices, extracting details, forwarding approvals, and updating records across multiple tools. This creates slow turnaround times, approval bottlenecks, inconsistent records, and avoidable manual errors.

## What This Does
- Receives invoice data or invoice submission trigger
- Extracts key invoice information for processing
- Uses Groq AI to structure and interpret finance data where needed
- Routes invoices through approval logic automatically
- Sends notifications to the right approvers
- Updates records after approval decisions
- Reduces repetitive manual work in finance operations

## Workflow Overview
The workflow starts when a new invoice is submitted or captured. It then extracts invoice details, applies structured processing rules, sends the invoice through approval routing, and logs the outcome. Notifications can be sent to finance stakeholders, and final results can be stored for audit and reporting purposes.

## Key Use Cases
- invoice intake automation
- finance approval routing
- accounts payable support
- invoice record updates
- internal finance process automation
- AI-assisted document and data handling
